# PRODUCT.md

## Register
Product — app UI serving a real verification workflow. Design serves clarity and trust, not spectacle.

## What this is
HireStack: a verified, structured staffing directory for overseas VA hiring into real estate
teams. Trust is the product — a candidate profile isn't visible to employers until every
flagged ambiguity in their employment history is resolved, by the candidate or by admin review.

## Primary users of this surface
**The Global Candidate** — an overseas VA going through resume upload, wizard Q&A, and admin
review gating. Often anxious: their livelihood depends on getting through this gate, English may
be a second language, connection may be slow/mobile, and the "why am I not visible yet" question
is high-stakes if left unanswered.

## Primary job on this flow
Get from sign-up to a fully published, structured profile in one sitting where possible; when
that's not possible (admin review needed), understand clearly *why* and that it's out of their
hands — not a mysterious dead end.

## Brand personality
Trustworthy, precise, calm. Never cutesy, never alarming. Every wait state should read as "the
system is working carefully on your behalf," not "something might be broken."

## Anti-references
- Not a generic SaaS-cream AI-slop onboarding wizard (gradient text, uppercase eyebrows, bouncy
  motion, hero-metric cards).
- Not consumer-gamified (no confetti, no XP bars, no forced positivity copy).
- Not enterprise-cold/dense (this is a single linear flow for one anxious user, not a dashboard).

## Accessibility
Candidates may be on slower connections/older devices, using a second language. State visibility
(loading, sending, uploading, error) must never rely on animation alone — always pair with text.
Contrast and touch targets matter; this may be used on mobile.

## Non-goals for this critique
No messaging/chat, no employer- or admin-side UI in scope here — candidate-facing flow only:
landing → sign-up/sign-in → resume upload → wizard → software confirm → done.
